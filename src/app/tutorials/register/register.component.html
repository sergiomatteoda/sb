Introduction

In this tutorial you will learn to register your SendBlaster Free Edition.

Why do I need to register my SendBlaster Free Edition?

Well, you may use SendBlaster Free edition for lifetime, that´s correct, there´s no time limitation. However, notice that after using SendBlaster Free edition for 7 days, you´re required to register and get a Free License.

Registering SendBlaster Free Edition

In order to Register SendBlaster Free Edition, please navigate to the official free registration page.

Make sure you fill all mandatory information, and check "I accept privacy conditions", then hit the confirm button.

You will then receive an email containing important information, and of course your Free License details that would look like:

Note: If you do not receive your Free license email, check on the spam box, just in case. You may also want to whitelist email address register@sendblaster.com.
If you still have problems getting your Free license, please Submit a trouble ticket.

Showing the License Details window

Every time SendBlaster starts, it shows a splash screen while it´s being initialized, hit the square button to display the License window.

Inserting Free License Details in SendBlaster

In this screen, you need to enter your Free license details that have been sent to you via email.

Email address used for registration: Enter the email address you used to register your SendBlaster Free License
License Key: Enter your Free License key. License key is a string of 5 sets of 4 numbers/letters, ie: 8685-686A-4862-7772-7C41

Note: It´s highly suggested that you copy/paste details from the registration email into the License window instead of typing this details, in order to avoid any typo. If the "Ok" button does not get enabled, it means that the license details are incorrect.

Verifying Registration

Verify that your Free License details appear in SendBlaster Splash screen. Now, you´re ready to go!
